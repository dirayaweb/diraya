Welcome to Diraya

Your Personal Food Safety Companion

Discover a smarter way to shop with confidence! Simply scan product barcodes to instantly check for allergens and make informed food choices.

How It Works

1. Scan the Barcode

Open your camera and scan the barcode on any food product.

2. Get Instant Results

The app will analyze the product and check for allergens based on your preferences.

3. Stay Safe & Shop Smart

If it's safe, you're good to go! If not, you'll get a warning with allergen details.
