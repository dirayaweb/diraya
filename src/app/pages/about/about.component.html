How It Works

1. Scan the Barcode

Open your camera and scan the barcode on any food product.

2. Get Instant Results

The app will analyze the product and check for allergens based on your preferences.

3. Stay Safe & Shop Smart

If it's safe, you're good to go! If not, you'll get a warning with allergen details.

Key Features

  • ✅ Instant Barcode Scanning – Get real-time allergen detection.
  • ✅ Personalized Alerts – Set your allergens and get customized warnings.
  • ✅ Community Contribution – Help improve the database by adding missing products.
  • ✅ Safe & Reliable – Powered by verified food data sources.
